Make Your Own Pilau Masala

Ingridients

  • 2 bay-leaves
  • tablespoon of cloves
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon of cardamom
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
  • 1/2 tablespoon of black pepper

Procedure

  1. In a bowl crash the bay-leaves until they turn into powder
  2. In another bowl crush the cardamom until they turninto powder
  3. In an order of your choosing add all the ingridients in a bowl
  4. Mix the powdered ingridients together until they become fine
  5. Place the mixture in a dry pan
  6. Roast over low heat until warmed through and fragrant and blend until smooth
  7. Your pilau masala is ready to go
  8. These measurements are enough to make about 3-4 cups of rice. However this recipe uses 2 cups of rice
  9. You can choose to double or triple the amounts to make a bigger batch store it into an airtight container for future use
